Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us.

Effective Date: October 2, 2025

1. Information We Collect

We collect the minimum amount of information necessary to provide our service:

  • Account Data: Username, hashed password (secured with bcrypt), and your chosen name color. We cannot access your original password.
  • User Activity: Messages sent in public rooms, private messages, and room membership.
  • Technical Data: IP address, session data (managed by express-session), and device information for security and abuse prevention.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the data we collect exclusively for the following purposes:

  • To facilitate login and maintain your secure session.
  • To deliver your messages and manage your profile customizations (name color, bio).
  • To improve the app's performance and troubleshoot technical issues.
  • For security, including the moderation of content and prevention of fraud or harassment.

3. Data Storage and Security

All user data and messages are stored in a PostgreSQL database. We employ industry-standard practices, including secure hashing for passwords and secure socket communication (Socket.io) for messages, to protect your data against unauthorized access.

4. Disclosure of Information

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your personally identifiable information to outside parties. Your information will only be disclosed if required by law or in the event of platform abuse.

5. Your Consent

By registering and using Vybez, you consent to our Privacy Policy.
